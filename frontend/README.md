# SmartVigilant

**The world's first dual-purpose AI security app** — protecting both your digital life and physical safety.

Built in 2026 with cutting-edge AI:
- Real-time malware & agentic AI threat detection
- Deepfake and phishing protection
- Live surveillance with motion/face/behavior AI
- Natural disaster early warnings
- Emergency panic button
- AI-vs-AI autonomous defense
- Privacy-first federated learning

## Features
- Beautiful dark theme with electric accents
- Real-time WebSocket alerts
- Live camera streaming with AI overlays
- Plain-language AI explanations
- Cross-platform (iOS, Android, Desktop)
