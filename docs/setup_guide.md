# SmartVigilant Setup Guide

Welcome to **SmartVigilant** — your AI-powered guardian for home and digital safety.

Follow these steps to get protected in under 5 minutes.

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Download the app from App Store or Google Play
2. Open and tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and create a strong password
4. Check your email and **verify your account** (required for security)

### Step 2: Connect Your First Camera
1. Tap **+ Add Device** on the dashboard
2. Select **Camera**
3. Choose your method:
   - **Scan QR code** (recommended)
   - **Enter RTSP URL manually** (for advanced users)
4. Give your camera a name (e.g., "Front Door")
5. Tap **Save** — AI will begin monitoring immediately

### Step 3: Set Safe Zones (Optional but Recommended)
1. Go to camera settings
2. Tap **Safe Zones**
3. Draw areas you want monitored (driveway, backyard)
4. Ignore zones (e.g., tree branches that move in wind)

### Step 4: Add Trusted Family Members
1. Go to **Settings → Family**
2. Tap **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email — they’ll get instant access and alerts

### Step 5: Enable Premium Protection (Optional)
- Tap **Upgrade** on dashboard
- Choose **Plus** or **Family** plan
- Unlock unlimited cameras, advanced AI insights, and priority support

### You're Protected!
Your system is now live with:
- Real-time deepfake detection
- Motion + person alerts
- Cyber threat scanning
- Autonomous defense

**Pro Tip**: Test with a wave at the camera — you should get a friendly "Person detected" alert.

Need help? Tap the **?** icon or email support@smartvigilant.com
