# SmartVigilant FAQ

### Is my privacy protected?
Yes — 100%. 
- Camera streams never leave your device unless you enable cloud backup
- Face recognition happens on-device
- Federated learning sends only anonymous model updates
- We use post-quantum encryption for all sensitive data

### Does it work offline?
Core features (motion, face, deepfake detection) work fully offline.
Cyber protection and alerts require internet.

### What cameras are supported?
Any camera with RTSP stream:
- Reolink, Amcrest, Hikvision, TP-Link, Wyze (with RTSP firmware)
- ONVIF-compatible devices
- Android/iOS phone as camera (via companion app)

### Why do I need email verification?
To prevent fake accounts and ensure only you control your home security.

### Can I use it without subscription?
Yes! Free plan includes:
- 1 camera
- Basic motion alerts
- Cyber threat scanning
- Panic button

Premium adds unlimited devices, advanced AI, and family sharing.

### What happens when a threat is detected?
1. AI double-checks
2. Autonomous response (e.g., block malicious IP)
3. You get instant push notification
4. Full log in Alert History

Email: support@smartvigilant.com  
Response within 24 hours (4 hours for Premium users)

We're here to keep you safe.
